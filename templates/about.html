Beta Programs

Fliche Wallet

The developer teams are currently working on the fliche wallet which will ensure high level security of user's funds.

P2P Transactions

The Peer-2-Peer feature will allow users to communicate with one another and trade digital assets successfully without risk of scams.

Brokerage

The brokerage feature will enable users to trade their assets, so you won't just have assets sitting in your wallet, you will be able to invest them as well with a Trading View chart and tons of asset classes to trade.
